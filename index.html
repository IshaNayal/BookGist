The Fault In Our Stars
In The Fault in Our Stars, sixteen-year-old Hazel Grace Lancaster is living with terminal cancer, spending her days reading and trying to stay out of the spotlight. But her world changes when she meets Augustus Waters at a support group—an intelligent, funny, and charismatic boy who’s also a cancer survivor. Their connection is instant, and together they embark on a journey that takes them to Amsterdam to meet Hazel’s favorite author. As their love grows, so do the challenges of illness and mortality. Heartbreaking yet beautifully hopeful, John Green’s novel explores love, loss, and what it means to truly live. It’s a story that will make you laugh, cry, and remember it long after you finish reading.
The Fault In Our Stars
In The Fault in Our Stars, John Green tells the unforgettable story of Hazel Grace Lancaster, a sixteen-year-old girl living with thyroid cancer that has spread to her lungs. Hazel lives a quiet life, often alone with her thoughts and her favorite novel, until she meets Augustus Waters at a cancer support group. Augustus is witty, charming, and determined to live life to the fullest despite having lost a leg to osteosarcoma. The two quickly form a deep connection, sharing jokes, philosophical conversations, and a love of books. Their bond leads them on an extraordinary trip to Amsterdam, where they hope to find answers from Hazel’s reclusive favorite author. But as they fall in love, they’re forced to confront the brutal realities of illness and the fragility of life. Both heartbreaking and filled with moments of humor and warmth, The Fault in Our Stars explores love, mortality, and what it means to leave a mark on the world. It’s a deeply emotional story that will make you laugh, cry, and think about life in a whole new way.

It Ends With Us
“It Ends With Us” by Colleen Hoover is an unforgettable, deeply moving novel that blends romance with raw emotional truth. The story follows Lily Bloom, a young woman who has worked hard to build the life she wants, leaving behind a difficult childhood in Maine. In Boston, she meets Ryle Kincaid, a brilliant and assertive neurosurgeon who seems perfect—except for his aversion to relationships. As Lily falls deeper into Ryle’s world, her first love, Atlas Corrigan, reappears, bringing back memories of a past that shaped her and challenging everything she thought she knew about love and loyalty. This is not just a love story—it’s a poignant exploration of the complexities of domestic abuse, resilience, and the painful choices we make to protect ourselves and those we love. With Hoover’s gripping storytelling and heartfelt characters, “It Ends With Us” will keep you turning pages late into the night—and leave you thinking about it long after the last sentence.

It Starts With Us
“It Starts With Us” picks up where It Ends With Us left off, diving back into the life of Lily Bloom as she tries to rebuild her world after heartbreak. Just as she’s finding her footing as a single mom, her first love, Atlas Corrigan, reappears, bringing with him hope, old memories, and the possibility of a future they never got to have. But moving forward isn’t simple—especially when the past still casts long shadows. With Colleen Hoover’s trademark blend of raw emotion, romance, and real-life stakes, It Starts With Us is a powerful story of healing, second chances, and the courage to choose love on your own terms. Fans of It Ends With Us won’t want to miss this beautiful continuation.

The Silent Patient
In The Silent Patient, Alicia Berenson, a celebrated painter, shocks the world when she shoots her husband five times—and then never speaks another word. Locked away in a psychiatric facility, she remains an enigma, her silence fueling endless speculation. Enter Theo Faber, a determined psychotherapist obsessed with uncovering the truth behind Alicia’s silence. But as he digs deeper, he discovers dark secrets that will shatter everything he thought he knew. Gripping, unpredictable, and full of stunning twists, this psychological thriller will keep you guessing until the final page.
400 Days
Keshav, a former detective turned cybersecurity expert, is pulled into the desperate search for a missing teenage girl. Her mother, Alia, beautiful and troubled, seeks his help when the police give up hope. As Keshav digs into the girl’s mysterious disappearance, he uncovers secrets that threaten powerful people and blur the lines between love, obsession, and duty. Fast-paced, emotional, and full of unexpected turns, 400 Days is a gripping thriller from India’s bestselling author, Chetan Bhagat.
400 Days
In 400 Days, bestselling author Chetan Bhagat weaves a gripping tale of mystery, love, and secrets. Keshav Rajpurohit, an ex-cop turned cybersecurity expert, is struggling with heartbreak and boredom when Alia Arora, a glamorous yet distressed mother, walks into his office. Her teenage daughter, Siya, has been missing for months, and the police have closed the case, leaving her desperate and alone. As Keshav and his friend Saurabh start investigating, they peel back layers of secrets involving wealthy families, hidden relationships, and sinister motives. But the deeper they dig, the more dangerous the truth becomes. Will they find Siya before it’s too late? Fast-paced and full of unexpected twists, 400 Days is a thrilling ride through love, obsession, and the dark side of human nature that will keep you hooked until the very last page.
